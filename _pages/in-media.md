Outreach Efforts
======

* Cosmic Wonders Guest Speaker Series, January 2022 - May 2022
  * Invited speakers share knowledge about the universe with the New College and Sarasota communities. 

* Economics Symposium Series, August 2020 - May 2021
  * Invited speakers from universities and industry to speak on topics related to finance and economics.
